About Me

My name is Matt Weber. I am 38 years old and live in Mukilteo, WA with my wife and son. I was born in Kentucky and have lived in Florida, Kansas, Virginia, and now Washington. I have an MS in Mechanical Engineering and am now learning the ins and outs of web development to pursue a different career path. I've been interested in programming for a while and am excited to put it to use.

When I am not working I enjoy video games, movies, and playing with my son. I also like traveling. I haven't had many big trips lately but quick excursions to the countryside for some wine tasting are always fun. I also enjoy cider and whisky.
